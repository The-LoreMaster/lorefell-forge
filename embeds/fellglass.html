Stances

Stance tiers unlock at armor levels 2, 6, and 10. Stance effects only run while the matching charge is lit: Tier 1 with one charge, Tier 2 with two, Tier 3 with three. When your charges clear, the effects fall silent. The Tier 1 bonus applies to your attributes automatically while active.

⛨ Lorebounds

Bonds form at the Fell Forging or by investing Vigor or Wit during a rest, up to two. Form follows level: Familiar, then Companion at 4, Corsair at 8.

❋ Skills

Tap a circle to set rank. Gold circles come from your lineage, origin, or motivation and cannot be cleared. At five filled circles a skill can be mastered: ranks return to 0, a mastery diamond is earned, and identity circles fall away. First mastery in a skill grants a talent.
