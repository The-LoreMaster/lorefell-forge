How to write an item the night shift can actually run

The worker reads title and notes. Nothing else. It has CANON.md and STYLE.md, but no idea what you meant. Answer three questions in the notes:

  1. Where. Real file paths. Not "the afflictions page" but FellGuide/The Combat/Afflictions.md. If you don't know it, say what you know and let it search.
  2. What done looks like. Observable and checkable. Not "improve the descriptions" but "each card body under 240 characters, no clipped text at 375px."
  3. What not to touch. "Do not change card order. Do not touch lineage names."

One item, one sitting, one commit. If the notes need "and" between two unrelated outcomes, that's two items. Small items fail small.

Never ask it to write a mechanic. Rules, formulas, and numbers already have exact wording somewhere in the vault. Say where, or say "find it." A missing source page is a question, not a gap to fill.

Priority is sequence, not importance. Everything here is worth doing. P1 means first. If four things are P1, none are.

Canon items are never pushed unattended. The worker branches, pushes the branch, and blocks the item for your review.

Twelve item cap. A thirteenth means deleting something. Use Add on hold to capture an idea without authorizing it; held items don't count.
