The peoples of the Sphere already in the vault. Read one, then base a new lineage on it.

Stratum mode forges all three as a matched set, one world with its lineage and its Brand. The world's lineage and Brand fill in from your other tabs. One send puts the whole set forward.

The Lineage

A people of the Sphere. Narrative and roleplay first. The one rule it carries is a single bonus.

The people, not a person. The word "The" is added for you.
    Plus one to a single skill or attribute at character creation. The only mechanical effect a lineage has.

                      The Brand

                      The world's magic. This is the one type with a ruleset. A Brand is called in battle and wakes something in the world beneath.

                      The Brand itself, for example Gelidane.
                        What wielders call the working. The full form and a short form.
                        Where the power comes from, how it feels to call, and the price it asks.
                          One framing line. What calling it strong does to the enemy, and what calling it weak does to your own side. The caller's side is the favored side. The enemy is the bound side.
                            The effect on a roll of 1 or 6. A battle-wide effect that favors the caller's side and holds until the battle ends.
                              On a roll of 2 to 5, the bound side suffers an Affliction. Pick one from the canon, or describe a new one.
                                Three things the practice does away from a fight.
                                  An image of the Brand, optional. Up to 4 MB. JPG, PNG, or WEBP.
